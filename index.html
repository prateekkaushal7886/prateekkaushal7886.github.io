About Me

A few words about me

I am Vishal Kumar Singh, an undergraduate student of the department of mining engineering. I am from Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. I have spent almost three years in this beautiful place and a major part of my personality has been moulded here. So in gratitude, I wish to serve the community I reside in. Serving this community involves coming closer to it. I have always been a ground level person. So I interact with everyone at a personal basis. Besides, if you feel any positive connect with me, I will be more than happy to connect with you on any platform.
